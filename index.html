What We Do

One partner.
Three approaches.

Most distributors expanding into new markets have to assemble a team that doesn't talk to each other. Cypress Point closes that gap — covering the full lifecycle from market identification to facility occupancy with the same advisor present at every stage.
01

Find It

The right market before the right property. We start by evaluating whether the market is right for your operation — not just what's available.

  • Market demand analysis
  • Customer proximity mapping
  • Competitive landscape review
  • Site identification & screening
  • Infrastructure evaluation
  • Labor market assessment
02

Buy It

Your interests — not the seller's. We also acquire sale-only properties and structure leasebacks so distributors get the facility without tying up capital.

  • Acquisition strategy
  • Offer structuring & negotiation
  • Environmental due diligence
  • Title & survey coordination
  • Zoning & entitlement review
  • Closing coordination
03

Build It

A facility that fits from day one. Specified for how your operation actually runs — dock ratios, clear heights, yard space, eave heights, column spacing.

  • Facility programming & spec
  • Architect & GC selection
  • Design review & value engineering
  • Permitting coordination
  • Construction oversight
  • Occupancy transition

Our Company

One principal.
One focus.

Munroe has spent 30 years working at the intersection of industrial real estate and distribution operations. Before founding Cypress Point, he led the southern division of one of the nation's largest building materials distributors — managing hundreds of branch locations across multiple states, navigating every variation of industrial facility the market had to offer.

That experience made one thing clear: distributors expanding into new markets are chronically underserved by generalist brokers who don't understand the specific requirements of distribution facilities.

When you talk about eave heights, column spacing, rack layout, and yard efficiency — real estate teams lean in. That's the difference Cypress Point brings to every engagement.
