About Munroe
"Industrial distributors deserve a partner who understands their operation — not just their square footage."

Munroe has spent 30 years working at the intersection of industrial real estate and distribution operations. Before founding Cypress Point, he led the southern division of one of the nation's largest building materials distributors — managing hundreds of branch locations across multiple states, navigating every variation of industrial facility the market had to offer.

That experience made one thing clear: distributors expanding into new markets are chronically underserved by generalist brokers who don't understand the specific requirements of distribution facilities. Cypress Point was founded to close that gap.

The Process

Buy it.
Build it.

Three phases. One partner. A clear line from market decision to operational facility — with the same advisor present at every stage. Nothing gets lost between phases.

01
Find
Phase 01 — Market & Site Selection
The right market before the right property.

Most distributors start with available properties. We start by evaluating whether the market is right for your operation.

  • Market demand analysis
  • Customer proximity mapping
  • Competitive landscape review
  • Site identification & screening
  • Infrastructure evaluation
  • Labor market assessment
02
Buy
Phase 02 — Acquisition & Transaction
Your interests. Not the seller's.

We also acquire sale-only properties and structure leasebacks — so distributors get the facility without tying up capital.

  • Acquisition strategy
  • Offer structuring & negotiation
  • Environmental due diligence
  • Title & survey coordination
  • Zoning & entitlement review
  • Closing coordination
03
Build
Phase 03 — Development Advisory
A facility that fits from day one.

Facilities specified for how your operation actually runs — dock ratios, clear heights, yard space, eave heights, column spacing.

  • Facility programming & spec
  • Architect & GC selection
  • Design review & value engineering
  • Permitting coordination
  • Construction oversight
  • Occupancy transition
